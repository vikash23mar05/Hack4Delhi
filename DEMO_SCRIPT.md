## 🎬 WHAT HAPPENS WHEN YOU CLICK

1. **Click Ward in Table** → Detailed panel opens, all 6 views load
2. **Click "Show Formula"** → Formula expands with live weight breakdown
3. **Click "Show Details"** → Data confidence details unfold
4. **Hover on Source** → Tooltip shows indicators (e.g., "Heavy vehicles 35% of traffic")
5. **Click Action Card** → Expands to show SLA, dept, cost, assign button
6. **Click "Assign to MCD"** → Task created (in real deployment)
7. **Click 24h/7d** → Chart redraws with extended history
8. **Click Complaint** → Expands to show update/evidence buttons
